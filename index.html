About Me

My full name is Neil Angelo L. Almanzor but most people call me Neil or Neil-o. I am a 19 year old colllege student of Malayan Colleges Mindanao. The course that I am currently taking is Entertainment and Multimedia Computing and am hoping to one day create a game of my own. While I do enjoy illustrating, I often find myself being too shy to share on platfroms such as Twitter and Instragam bacause of the toxicity that sometimes plague those platforms. Nevertheless, I still do enjoy creating artworks.

It's also a dream of mine to be a webtoon creator, but I am still learning the ins and outs of that industry. I am an avid fan of anything Type-moon related as well as amazing animations (Specifically the works of Ufotable such as Kara no Kyoukai and the Fate Series). My hobbies includes drawing fan arts, watching animated shows on netflix and other media, and playing games from different genres such as metroid-vania, rpg, gacha, and mmo. On top of that, I also enjoy doing pokemon challenges such as Pokemon Nuzlockes and Single Type challenges. One weird thing I do that I can happily share is that I multitask 2-3 gacha games at a time depending on the situation and those games being Granblue Fantasy, Fate/Grand Order, and Genshin Impact.

Future Goals

My goals in life have always been the same ever since I was introducted to drawing. I wanted to create a webtoon that hopefully becomes popular as well as create an indie game of my own under the genre of metroid-vania. 30 years in the future I want to become someone who's had a good artistic career path and is working on a game company.
