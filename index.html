About Me

My full name is Neil Angelo L. Almanzor but most people call me Neil or Neil-o. I am a 19 year old colllege student of Malayan Colleges Mindanao. The course that I am currently taking is Entertainment and Multimedia Computing and am hoping to one day create a game of my own. Since I have been exposed to Japanese traditions and media because of my time in Philippine Nikke Jin Kai International School, I have been drawing for a very long time. It's also a dream of mine to be a webtoon creator, but I am still learning the ins and outs of that industry. I am an avid fan of anything Type-moon related as well as amazing animations (Specifically the works of Ufotable such as Kara no Kyoukai, Fate Series and Kimetsu no Yaiba). My hobbies includes drawing fan arts, watching animated shows on netflix and other media, and playing games from different genres such as metroid-vania, rpg, gacha, and mmo. On top of that, I also enjoy doing pokemon challenges such as Pokemon Nuzlockes and Single Type challenges. One weird thing I do that I can happily share is that I multitask 2-3 gacha games at a time depending on the situation and those games being Granblue Fantasy, Fate/Grand Order, and Genshin Impact.
